Tourist Destinations

Explore the must-visit places in Kerala

Kovalam Beach

A famous crescent-shaped beach with lighthouse views.

Varkala Cliff

The only seaside cliff formation in Kerala.

Ponmudi Hills

A misty hill station with winding roads and greenery.

Padmanabhaswamy Temple

An iconic Vishnu temple with Dravidian architecture.

Thiruvananthapuram Zoo

One of the oldest zoos in India, set in a botanical garden.
